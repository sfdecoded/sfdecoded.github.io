Salesforce Developer & Creator

The Story Behind Salesforce Decoded

I started my journey creating SFDC Saga, sharing interactive LWC components and Salesforce insights with the community. Then came Salesforce Revamp, a Chrome extension to speed up development workflows. But I kept running into the same problem — developers were spending hours searching for answers to common Salesforce questions and errors.

I realized the real need wasn't another tool or extension. It was a single place where developers could find practical solutions, curated prompts, and reliable guidance. That's why I built Salesforce Decoded. Every feature exists because I've felt the frustration developers face daily. Today, thousands use it every month to work smarter and faster.

Our Mission

We believe Salesforce developers deserve better tooling. The platform is powerful but the developer experience can be frustrating — cryptic error messages, scattered documentation, and repetitive boilerplate code.

Salesforce Decoded exists to bridge that gap. Every tool, guide, and error solution we publish is designed to save you time and reduce friction in your daily workflow.
